WHO WE ARE

We are a group of eight students from IIS Badoni in Lecco who have set themselves the goal of speeding up the days and enhance the time of our customers through the smart wearable devices we produce. By using them it will be possible to authenticate using specially made sensors, saving all the time needed to look for keys, weave or remove gloves when using motor vehicles. Our goal is to bring the products not only into private homes, but also in companies and in general in the public sector.
